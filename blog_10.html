Structural & Design Integration Analysis

The 16x16 Round Smart LED Mirror introduces a high-grade aesthetic upgrade specifically designed for modern washbasins, compact bathroom layouts, and vanity corners. Built with clear HD silvered glass, it minimizes optical distortion while maximizing ambient light scatter across wet room surfaces.

Equipped with an integrated touch-sensor system directly etched on the mirror face, users can instantly cycle through three deliberate color temperature channels—Cool White, Natural White, and Warm White—tailoring optimal lighting for skincare, precision grooming, or soft nighttime lighting.

Engineering Advantages

  • Tri-Tone Color Architecture: Effortlessly switches between 6500K (Cool), 4000K (Natural), and 3000K (Warm) output levels.
  • Space-Optimized 16x16 Form Factor: Fits seamlessly over compact wash basins, guest powder rooms, and small vanities.
