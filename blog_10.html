Structural Integration Analysis

The 16x16 Inch Round Wall-Mounted LED Mirror delivers an optimal design transition for modern residential wash basins and luxury powder rooms. Engineered around high-grade float glass with an anti-corrosion silver coating layer, this mirror prevents moisture-induced edge oxidation common in standard bathroom fixtures.

At its core lies a high-lumen, energy-efficient LED matrix featuring three distinct color temperatures: Cool White (6500K) for high-precision grooming, Natural White (4000K) for neutral reflection, and Warm White (3000K) for relaxed ambient lighting. The integrated smart touch sensor offers smooth dimming and quick toggling even with wet hands.

Engineering Advantages

  • 3-Tone LED Core: Seamlessly switches between Cool, Natural, and Warm color spaces.
  • Hydro-Shield Build: Sealed internal electronics designed specifically for high-humidity wash basin zones.
  • Smart Touch Interface: Responsive touch control key integrated directly into the glass surface.
  • Distortion-Free: Precision ground float glass eliminates focal warp across all viewing angles.
